Overview

Purpose

The CC store as the name suggests is a store that sells computers, laptops, computer hardware, and software. The store will not only be venturing into the selling business but will also be doing all kinds of computer repairs. Types of computer hardware that will be sold in the store include Desktop computers, Laptops, monitors, mice, keyboards, computer speakers, soundcards, RAMS, Hardrives, Motherboards, SATA cables of all types, etc. Types of software that will be sold in the store include Microsft 365 office suite, Office 2019 office suite, and Anti-virus software such as Trend Micro, Bitdefender, Norton, Kaspersky, etc. Other types of software such as accounting software packages, order tracking software, off-site and on-site backup, Email marketing solutions, diagnostic, All versions of Microsoft Windows 10, and 11, etc.

Audience

Customers will be able to get all services online, they will also be able to make orders and have them delivered to their desired location. Local customer will have their orders safely delivered within 24hrs guarantee. Customers will also be allowed to bring their computers to the store for repairs and they will be able to buy spare parts in the store as well should a need arise. The store will be operating 6 days a week from Monday all the way to Saturday. Normal working hours a day will be from 08:00 AM to 06:PM until Friday, then on weekends working hours will change a little as the shop opens from 09:00 AM to 01:30 PM. The store will be located inside the town so that it will be central to almost all customers as well as other businesses who may need Cabang's Computer Store services.

Page 1: Home page

I believe people will want to visit the website for quite a number of reasons such as if they desire to buy an office or personal computer. Living in the 21st century it’s almost impossible not to own a computer device. We have different types of computer gadgets laptops, desktop computers, smartphones, tablets, etc. Hence is another reason why will want to visit the website. People will also visit the site should they run into problems with their computer whether is a personal computer or it’s a business computer, they will have to opportunity to make bookings to bring their computers to the shop for repair, as well as upgrades. Other people will visit the website to buy software such the anti-virus, business software such as past, windows operating systems. These are quite the main reasons among many more as to why will people visit the Cabang's Computer Store.

The website will be user-friendly so that customers will have all the crucial information at their disposal, and this will help decrease the number of calls from customers that need specific information regarding whatever they may need or want. Customers will be able to reach us by calling the store telephone line that will be available on the website they will also be able to reach to through e-mail.

Page 2: Software’s

Software that will be sold in the store will include the popular Microsoft 365 office suite, and Office 2019 office suite suitable for both personal and business use. We will also be selling computer anti-virus software which will include Trend Micro as our customer-recommended anti-virus. The Bitdefender will be the second customer-recommended anti-virus followed by the Norton and lastly Kaspersky. Other types of software such as accounting software packages like the pastel payroll system, order tracking software, off-site and on-site backup, Email marketing solutions, diagnostic, All versions of Microsoft Windows 10, and 11, etc.

Remote IT support we are able to support our customers via the web using VPN, VNC, team, and other support remote applications. For small to large enterprises, we do offer computer support to our valued customers, through the relationships built over time with valued customers where they will be offered support contracts with CCS for continuous support. We will also manage server installation and maintenance and help the business with backup critical information that might cause the business to shut down should it be lost. This will help the business to focus more on running the business as a whole and live the IT infrastructure to capable hands to run that side of the business.
